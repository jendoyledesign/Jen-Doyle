CSX

Through my internship at Exit 10, I worked on an enthusiastic team of designers to create an exhibiton for CSX. As an intern I developed all the icons usd throughout the booths and handouts. CSX, a national leader in the transportation industry, looks to Exit10 to help them make a positive impact there each year. In 2014, CSX wanted to showcase its role in food transport. We developed an interactive booth, where visitors could follow two tracks that detailed CSX's part in the making of corn into food and animal feed. We then used #EveryKernelCounts to get students involved in fighting hunger in the U.S.
