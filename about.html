Hiya!

Jen Doyle is a graphic designer based in Washington DC. She is originally from New Jersey but has also lived across the pond. She received her Bachelor of Arts Degree in Graphic Design from the Maryland Institute College of Art (MICA). She currently works at Illustria and has previously interned at Exit 10 and BOPA. She was also a PNC Design Fellow during the summer of 2014 where she helped rebrand local non-profit organizations. In her free time she enjoys documenting her adventures, reading magazines and playing music. Check out her current project, Brazen. A feminist arts and culture magazine empowering women artists.
