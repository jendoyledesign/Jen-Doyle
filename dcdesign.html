DC Design Week 2017

DC Design Week is led and produced by AIGA DC, the local chapter of the professional association for design. AIGA advances design as a professional craft, strategic advancement, and vital cultural force. As a designer, I focused on developing a site that promoted the events during the week long celebration by integrating illustrations by JD Deardroff and the DC Design Week brand art directed by Dan Rader.
