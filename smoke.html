Smoke App

Trofi Ventures is developing an app called SMOKE Chat. This app is a secure messenger designed for people who need extra security and protection for their phone messages. Every message sent through S.M.O.K.E has the ability to self destruct through the use of a built in timer. This app was developed with a sleek black on black concept to create a professional, trustworthy and high tech looking product that is also user friendly.
